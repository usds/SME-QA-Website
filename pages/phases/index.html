When you've decided to use the SME-QA process to find qualified applicants, you can plan your hiring action.

Assessment Strategy

Describe your intended process for each phase of your hiring action.

Job analysis workshop

Conduct a two-day job analysis workshop with HR and SMEs to develop your assessments.

Job Announcement

Post an accurate and compelling job announcement to attract the most qualified applicants.

Resume Review

Two SMEs review each resume against the core competencies and proficiencies.

Phone Interviews

SMEs conduct up to two rounds of phone interviews to determine which applicants meet the minimum qualifications.

Issuing certificate

HR applies veterans' preference and applies category ratings.
